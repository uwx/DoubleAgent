Double Agent API

The BalloonStyleFlags Enumeration
&H00000001 = Enabled The word balloon is enabled.
&H00000002 = SizeToText The word balloon is automatically resized to fit the speech or thought text.
&H00000004 = AutoHide The word balloon is hidden automatically after a short delay.
&H00000008 = AutoPace The text in the word balloon is shown one word at a time.
&H00000010 = ShowPartialLines Partial lines are shown when the balloon text scrolls (for a word balloon that's not SizeToText).
