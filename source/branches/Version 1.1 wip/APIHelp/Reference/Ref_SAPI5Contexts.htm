SAPI5 English Normalization Contexts

The following normalization contexts are defined by SAPI5, but not guaranteed to be implemented, for English speech engines.

Context Description Details
date The text is normalized as a date

Dates will generally have the format of number [delimiter] number [delimiter] number or number [delimiter] number where the delimiter can be a '.', '/' or '-', and numbers are typically between 01 and 12 for months, 01 and 31 for days. A year is generally a two or four-digit number.
date_mdy A date in m/d/y format

<CONTEXT ID="date_mdy">12/21/99</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December twenty first ninety nine" or "December twenty first nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_mdy">12/21/1999</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December twenty first nineteen ninety nine"
date_dmy A date in d/m/y format

<CONTEXT ID="date_dmy">21.12.99</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December twenty first ninety nine" or "December twenty first nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_ dmy">21-12-1999</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December twenty first nineteen ninety nine"
date_ymd A date in y/m/d format

<CONTEXT ID="date_ymd">99-12-21</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December twenty first ninety nine" or "December twenty first nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_ ymd">1999.12.21</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December twenty first nineteen ninety nine"
date_ym A date in y/m format

<CONTEXT ID="date_ym">99-12</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December ninety nine" or "December nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_ ym">1999.12</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December nineteen ninety nine"
date_my A date in m/y format

<CONTEXT ID="date_my">12/99</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December ninety nine" or "December nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_my">12/1999</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "December nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_dm">21.12</CONTEXT>

date_md A date in m/d format

<CONTEXT ID="date_md">12/21</CONTEXT>

date_year A numeric year

<CONTEXT ID="date_year">1999</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "nineteen ninety nine"

<CONTEXT ID="date_year">2001</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "two thousand one"
time The text is normalized as a time

Times will generally have the format of number [delimiter] number [delimiter] number or number [delimiter] number where the delimiter is ':' or ' ' ' or ' " ' and numbers are typically between 01 and 24 for hours, 01 and 59 for minutes and seconds.

<CONTEXT ID="time">12:30</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "twelve thirty"

<CONTEXT ID="time">01:21</CONTEXT>
is normalized as "one twenty one"

<CONTEXT ID="time">1'21"</CONTEXT>
is normalized as "one minute twenty one seconds" or "one minute and twenty one seconds"
number_cardinal The text is normalized as a number using the regular format of ones, tens, etc.

<CONTEXT ID="number_cardinal">3432</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "three thousand four hundred thirty two" or "three thousand four hundred and thirty two"
number_digit The text is normalized digit by digit.

<CONTEXT ID="number_digit">3432</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "three four three two"
number_fraction The text is normalized as a fraction.

<CONTEXT ID="number_fraction">3/15</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "three fifteenths" or "three over fifteen"
number_decimal The text is normalized as a decimal value.

<CONTEXT ID="number_decimal">423.1243</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "four hundred and twenty three point one two four three"
phone_Number The text is normalized as a phone number.

The exact implementation is left to the engine developer and may be defined in a future release of SAPI.
currency The text is normalized as a currency.

<CONTEXT ID="currency">$34.90</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "thirty four dollars and ninety cents"
web_url The text is normalized as a URL.

<CONTEXT ID="web_url">www.microsoft.com</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "w w w dot microsoft dot com"
e-mail The text is normalized as e-mail.

The exact implementation is left to the engine developer and may be defined in a future release of SAPI.
e-mail_address The text is normalized as an e-mail address.

<CONTEXT ID="e-mail_address">someone@microsoft.com</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "someone at microsoft dot com"
address The text is normalized as an address.

<CONTEXT ID="address">One Microsoft Way, Redmond, WA, 98052</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "One Microsoft Way Redmond Washington nine eight zero five two"
address_postal The text is normalized as a postal address.

<CONTEXT ID="address_postal">A2C 4X5</CONTEXT>
is normalized to "A 2 C 4 X 5"
